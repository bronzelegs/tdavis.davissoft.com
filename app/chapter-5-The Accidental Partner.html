Chapter 5 — The Accidental Partner

Fifth Avenue in fall after a rainfall was a thing of beauty. This afternoon they’d had enough of a shower to make the street sparkle in the headlights.

They passed corner after corner crowded with people in a rush to be somewhere warm and dry. The cold gave the evening an edge and kept the couples out for dinner huddled close.

The sound of water splashing against the taxis where water had gathered in places at corners and clogged rain sewers interrupted the silence.

He turned his head to look at her.

She began “What have you been up to lately? I’ve been so busy with the internship I sort of lost track of the study group.”

“Oh, I’m working on more unmarketable letters after my name. The challenge of pursuing a career that has no jobs; either settle for something you are just a little overqualified for or keep going, which makes you a lot overqualified.”

“Oh, I know. “

“How about you, still political science?”

“Sort of. I’m hoping my internship leads to something great. I want to stay here in the city, I love the bustle.”

“I’ve been doing the interview circuit, and hoping to wrap up my dissertation in the spring.”

She didn’t want to talk about work. “Any prospects you are hopeful about?”

He smiled, “I’ve been doing this interview thing which I might pan out, it’s a real long shot for sure though. Which is sort of..”

“Oh? Tell me about it.”

“It’s posting on InLink. A really unusual posting for a job on with a startup. Jake has been helping here and there.“

“Like what kind of a startup?”

He smiled “ A real mixed bag of wave theory and chemistry questions and exercises.“

“And I must have passed because they sent me an email inviting me to a treasure hunt. So I’m thinking like a little adventure, you know a culture kind of thing.”

“Go on. So what do you need to do?”

“Simple stuff, recover some objects, follow instructions and just go with the flow, blah blah. It’s like 3 things. ”

“Sounds kind of fun. ”

“It’s on the way, sort of, If you want to, I mean if you’re into it. It might take a while, but its simple stuff.”

“So sure, we can do that.” She was smiling. “When can we start ?”

“We can start now. First thing is this address on Fox over by the freeway. Find this office, look for clues.”

“Let’s go.”

“Ok driver, we’ll get out here please.”
